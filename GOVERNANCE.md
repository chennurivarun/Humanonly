## Constitutional Rules
1. No AI-authored user posts.
2. No irreversible account-level enforcement without human review.
3. All material moderation actions must be auditable.
4. Appeals are guaranteed.
5. Emergency human override is always available.

## Decision Matrix
- **AI-only**: queue prioritization, spam throttles, risk scoring.
- **AI recommend + Human approve**: takedowns, suspensions, major trust penalties.
- **Human-only**: permanent bans, policy changes, legal escalations.
